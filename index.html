CNS (Centralized News Source) is your new home for legitimate news. No more fake news! We are a community that gathers only 100% true news stories from all over the world and put them on our site for your viewing.

The Problem

Fake news is the product of websites deliberately pushing false stories, hoaxes and conspiracy theories, and the American public has difficulty differentiating fake news articles from the real ones. Fake news has been catapulted into the mainstream throughout the last year, especially during the 2016 presidential election. The perception of these fake websites used to be those that deliberately push false stories, hoaxes and conspiracy theories but now include pretty much any claim of dubious nature. A recent study conducted by professors at NYU, Stanford, and the National Bureau of Economic Research (NBER) found that about 6/10 U.S. adults read fake news stories, specifically pertaining to politics. The study also suggested that fake news might have been pivotal in the election of President Trump. The American public now has difficulty trusting real news sites because the sitting president calls any news organization that is critical of him, fake news. There are various articles online just on how to differentiating real news from fake news.

The Solution

Unfortunately, there are many websites on the World Wide Web with fake/falsely advertised news. With this re-occurring issue, misinformation is spread at an alarming rate, causing mass confusion to the public. These websites confuse many individuals who are seeking knowledge on various issues/topics or those who want to stay updated on current issues. Centralized News Source is here to change the way we get access to accurate news! Centralized News Source is a site where you are able to get all your credible news sources. Our website filters out false news stories to provide a place where individuals can rely on trustworthy news. All of our articles, news stories, journals, etc. are all completely reliable. Our mission is to accurately inform individuals with authentic, fact-based stories. We believe that it is imperative to provide our audience with honesty and with this foundation, we are able to achieve this mission. We are proud to introduce Centralized News Source and we are optimistic that this site will provide many individuals with a sense of reassurance and confidence that the information that they are processing is legitimate and authentic.

Our Plan

We have a dedicated team here at CNS to bring you legitimate news. When a news story is released (from Fox, CNN, NBC, etc.) our team reviews the content and publishes it once it has been proven to be true. We do not own or take credit for any of the news stories we post and that is why we send you directly to the provider of the story. Fake news has started to get out of hand and that is why we have decided to make a difference. We guarantee any link posted on our website will be nothing but the truth.
